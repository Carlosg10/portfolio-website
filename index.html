Aspirations

My aspiration after becoming a paid developer run far. After getting paid for my code I would like to learn on for about 5 more years and than start working for myself. I would love to teach grade school kids how to code because I believe learning this skill at an early age is really benefiting. I would also like to develop an app people can use on their phone to make their nyc public parking experience convenient and free of expense of private parking lot fees.
